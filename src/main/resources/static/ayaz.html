About Ayaz

Built by people who ran a rental shop.

Ayaz started in 2024 in Istanbul. The founders ran a 14-car operation in Kadıköy for six years and got tired of the spreadsheet-and-WhatsApp routine. So they built the tool they wished they'd had.

Today, over a hundred rental companies — from single-branch family shops to multi-city operators — use Ayaz to manage their fleet, talk to customers and grow their business. We've stayed focused on the boring parts: reliability, fair pricing, real human support.
